Why Choose Us?

Experience :

First : The hairdressers in the salon are carefully and meticulously selected,
whether in terms of skill and experience in the profession or in terms
of behavior and ethics. In addition to the staff who are trained in the salon.

Cleanliness:

Second:We excluded all the tools that cause the transmission of
infectious diseases from the salon, starting with the chin brush, which is a fertile environment
for the growth of germs and bacteria, and ending with cutting, drying and cleaning pads.

Time Saving :

A solution to the problem of wasting time for the clients of the place, we have
created schedules so that the customer chooses his spare time and comes to the salon
to find the barber waiting for him and start shaving as soon as the customer arrives.
